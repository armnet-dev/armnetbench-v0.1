Read the results carefully

v0.1 validated the system.
It did not remove every confound.

Limitations reported in the paper
  • Each task ran on one physical cell, so cross-cell agreement was not measured.
  • Object placement drifted across evaluation days and two failed resets were removed.
  • Task objects deteriorated during evaluation.
  • One front camera was misaligned and one bimanual wrist camera was slightly blurry.
  • Lighting was ambient rather than calibrated across cells.
